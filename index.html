System Components

Four Integrated Research Modules

Each component addresses a distinct dimension of driver safety, and together they form a unified intelligent system for real-time monitoring and alerting.

😠

Emotion Shift Profile Analysis

IT22608222 · Weerakkody D.S.N

Monitors driver facial expressions using a CNN trained on FER-2013 to compute a novel Behavioural Volatility Index (BVI) that quantifies emotional stability across a rolling 30-frame window. BVI combines transition rate, emotion variance, and Shannon entropy to classify drivers as stable, unstable, or erratic.

Keras CNN Haar Cascade BVI Metric FER-2013 MongoDB
😴

Reliability-Aware Drowsiness Detection

IT22115898 · Uddala W.U.P

A five-model progressive pipeline using EfficientNet-B0 with CNN→LSTM temporal fusion. Introduces a novel Reliability Gate mechanism that dynamically weights eye-region signals based on estimated quality, enabling robust multi-modal fusion even under occlusion or poor lighting.

EfficientNet-B0 LSTM Transformer MS-TCN Reliability Gate
🚸

Robust Road Sign Detection

IT22579218 · Hetti Thanthrige N.K

Detects and classifies road sign quality using a YOLO-based detector with SAHI slice-based augmentation for small sign detection. An ensemble of MobileNetV2, custom CNN, and YOLO classifiers provides robust sign condition assessment — normal vs. damaged — for Sri Lankan road conditions.

YOLOv8 SAHI MobileNetV2 Ensemble MJPEG Stream
🛣️

Hybrid Road Hazard Scoring

IT22189080 · Gunawardena S.T

Fuses semantic scene understanding (SegFormer with 16 safety classes) with map-based terrain analysis (GPS, SRTM elevation, route curvature) into a composite hazard score. A custom fusion neural network learns the relationship between visual scene risk and geographic map risk factors.

SegFormer SRTM Elevation Fusion MLP Flask + React WebSocket
Research Domain

Domain & Literature

Deep learning-based intelligent transportation systems for real-time driver behavioral analysis and road safety in Sri Lanka's public transport sector.

Literature Survey Background

Road traffic injuries remain the leading cause of death for persons aged 5–29 globally, with an estimated 1.19 million fatalities annually (WHO, 2023). In Sri Lanka, fatal bus crashes — such as the Gerandi Ella disaster (June 2025, 23 deaths) and the Badulla crash (September 2025, 15 deaths) — underscore the urgency of proactive driver monitoring. Existing systems focus on single modalities: either physiological sensors, vehicle telemetry, or isolated computer vision tasks. BusMate addresses this by integrating four complementary deep-learning modules into a unified, real-time safety platform covering emotion, alertness, sign recognition, and road hazard assessment.

Research Gap Gap Analysis

Current driver monitoring systems exhibit five critical gaps: (1) no composite metric for emotional volatility beyond simple emotion classification, (2) absence of reliability weighting in multi-modal drowsiness fusion, (3) limited road sign condition assessment under real-world degradation, (4) no integration of visual scene analysis with geographic terrain data for holistic hazard scoring, and (5) insufficient validation under Sri Lankan driving conditions. BusMate directly addresses each of these gaps through its four research components.

Research Problem Problem Statement

"How can multiple deep-learning-based driver behavior analysis modules — emotion profiling, drowsiness detection, road sign recognition, and scene-terrain hazard scoring — be integrated into a cohesive real-time system that provides proactive safety alerts for Sri Lankan public transport drivers, with statistically validated metrics and deployment-ready performance?"

Research Objectives Objectives

Emotion Shift Analysis

Develop and validate the BVI metric for real-time driver emotional volatility quantification using CNN-based facial emotion recognition.

Drowsiness Detection

Design a reliability-aware temporal fusion architecture for drowsiness detection with subject-independent evaluation on CPU hardware.

Road Sign Detection

Build a robust road sign detection and condition classification system using YOLO + SAHI + ensemble classifiers for Sri Lankan road conditions.

Road Hazard Scoring

Fuse SegFormer scene segmentation with SRTM terrain analysis into a composite road hazard scoring system with neural fusion.

Methodology Approach

The project follows an Agile Scrum methodology adapted for deep learning research, where each component represents a sprint with defined objectives, deliverables, and evaluation criteria. Development proceeds through iterative cycles of data collection, model training, evaluation, and refinement.

02

03

Integration

Flask/Node.js backend, React frontend, MongoDB, Socket.IO

04

Validation

Statistical tests, subject-independent evaluation, field testing
